Simple omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines the beaten of eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetable or meat.

Preparation time

  • Total: minutes
  • preparation: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optionally filling:chees, diced vegetables, cooked meat,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl,beat the egg with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. you can add a tablespoon of water or milk to give it a fluffier mixture
  2. Heat the pan:place the non-stick frying pan over the medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. Cook the omelette:once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the egg to the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the pan.
  4. Add filling (optional): when the eggs begins to set at the edges but are still slightly running in the midden, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve:as the omelettecontinue to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minutes,then slide it into a plate
  6. Enjoy:serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
